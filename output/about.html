About Us


Carolina Weather Group, LLC has provided quality weather forecasts, educational materials, and live severe weather coverage for the Carolinas since 2013. In that time, we've seen more than a dozen tropical cyclones, hundreds of tornadoes, and nearly every other form of extreme weather. We're currently nine members strong, with professionals in various private sector forecasting, broadcast, and academic positions.
